ST6 presets#

Combinations of calibrated coefficients for ST6 are defined in the SWAN Manual. The following presets are available:

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.ST6C1

First ST6 calibration in the SWAN user manual.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.ST6C2

Second ST6 calibration in the SWAN user manual.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.ST6C3

Third ST6 calibration in the SWAN user manual.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.ST6C4

Fourth ST6 calibration in the SWAN user manual.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.ST6C5

Fifth ST6 calibration in the SWAN user manual.

Transmission and reflection#

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.TRANSM

Constant transmission coefficient.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.TRANS1D

Frequency dependent transmission.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.TRANS2D

Frequency-direction dependent transmission.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.GODA

DAM transmission of Goda/Seelig (1979).

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.DANGREMOND

DAM transmission of d'Angremond et al. (1996).

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.REFL

Obstacle reflections.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.RSPEC

Specular reflection.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.RDIFF

Diffuse reflection.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.FREEBOARD

Freeboard dependent transmission and reflection.

rompy.swan.subcomponents.physics.LINE

Line of points to define obstacle location.
